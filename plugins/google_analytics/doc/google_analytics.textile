h1. Google Analytics

Tracking and web analytics for people and communities.

h2. Usage

Create and configure "Google Analytics":http://www.google.com/analytics/
account following these steps:

# Signin to Google Analytics
!=/plugins/google_analytics/images/doc/google-analytics-setup-step1.en.png(Google Setup - Step 1)!
# Fill in _Website's URL_ with your profile address (eg.: softwarelivre.org/joenio) and _Account Name_ with your name
!=/plugins/google_analytics/images/doc/google-analytics-setup-step2.en.png(Google Setup - Step 2)!
# Fill in your first and last name
!=/plugins/google_analytics/images/doc/google-analytics-setup-step3.en.png(Google Setup - Step 3)!
# Read and accept terms of use
!=/plugins/google_analytics/images/doc/google-analytics-setup-step4.en.png(Google Setup - Step 4)!
# Accept default options and click next
!=/plugins/google_analytics/images/doc/google-analytics-setup-step5.en.png(Google Setup - Step 5)!
# Take note of your Google Analytics Profile ID (eg.: UA-23455430-3)
!=/plugins/google_analytics/images/doc/google-analytics-setup-step6.en.png(Google Setup - Step 6)!
# Login to Noosfero and go to your control panel
!=/plugins/google_analytics/images/doc/google-analytics-setup-step7.en.png(Google Setup - Step 7)!
# Follow _Profile Info and Settings_ and fill in _Google Analytics Profile ID_ with ID above
